Pilehaven -
Willow Garden

56° 27' 14.2626"

9° 25' 1.7544"

Small park with plaited willows and maples, placed directly at the Nørresø.

The facility is located on land that was once owned by monks in the Middle Ages.

Several fruit trees from the old gardens remained standing and are today prominent in the park.

The name "Pilehaven" was given to the facility after 1995 - 96 , when the entire area underwent a major renovation.
