All the garden's herbs, shrubs and trees are mentioned in the New and Old Testament.

Bibelhaven is part of the 'Borgvold' park and was inaugurated in 1989

You can experience a unique collection of herbs, shrubs and trees.

All are mentioned in the Old Testament and the New Testament and provided with name tags, Bible quotations and often peppered with information about legends and superstitions.
