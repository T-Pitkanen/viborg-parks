The water landscape at Sønæs is a city park with many facilities.

Sønæs is a climate project with a focus on the environment, water purification, climate protection and much more.

At the nature area you will find, among other things:

  • Parking lot with infoportal
  • 725m long footpath
  • Sun stairs and beach
  • Pier for the boat "Margrethe I"
Read more about the facilities in the sØnæs folder.
